# Amanda Natividad

## Profile

**LinkedIn:** (https://www.linkedin.com/in/amandanat/)

**Area of Expertise:** Zero-click marketing, audience research, content strategy, demand generation, audience building, and B2B marketing.

## Overview

Amanda Natividad is the Vice President of Marketing at SparkToro and one of the leading advocates of Zero-Click Marketing. Her work focuses on helping marketers create content that delivers value directly on social platforms rather than forcing users to click away. She specializes in audience research, demand generation, and building trust through educational content. Her frameworks are particularly relevant for B2B SaaS companies seeking to grow organic reach and brand awareness in an environment where traditional click-through rates continue to decline.

## Post 2

**Title:** Crickets? Tumbleweeds? These Are Data Points

**Content Type:** LinkedIn Post

**Date:** Nov 2025

**Source:** [LinkedIn Profile](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_crickets-tumbleweeds-these-are-data-points-share-7377031828731252736-V9um/)

### Summary

Amanda explains that low engagement should not automatically be interpreted as failure. She argues that every content outcome provides useful feedback about audience interests, messaging, and content-market fit. Rather than chasing viral performance, marketers should use engagement data to refine future content and better understand their audience.

### Main Marketing Lesson

Content performance should be viewed as a learning process rather than a simple success-or-failure measurement.

### Why It Matters for B2B SaaS

B2B SaaS content often targets niche audiences, where engagement may appear modest despite delivering business value. Amanda's perspective helps marketers focus on long-term learning and audience development instead of short-term vanity metrics.
