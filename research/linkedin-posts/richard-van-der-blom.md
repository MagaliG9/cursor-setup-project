Richard shared findings from his annual LinkedIn Algorithm Insights research, analyzing large volumes of LinkedIn content to identify trends in reach, engagement, and content distribution. The report emphasizes the growing importance of expertise, audience relevance, and topical authority.

## Post 2

**Title:** Topic Fingerprinting and Content Authority 

**Content Type:** LinkedIn Post

**Date:** May 2026

**Source:** [LinkedIn Post] <https://www.linkedin.com/posts/richardvanderblom_rvdbar26ch1topic-fingerprint-ugcPost-7458040786761355264-cqkg/>

### Summary

Richard discussed how LinkedIn associates creators with specific topics based on publishing patterns and audience interactions. This concept, often referred to as topic authority, influences who sees a creator's content.

### Main Marketing Lesson

B2B SaaS marketers should consistently publish around a limited number of strategic content pillars instead of constantly changing topics.

### Why It Matters for B2B SaaS

B2B SaaS companies often struggle to stand out in crowded markets. The concept of topic fingerprinting highlights the importance of consistently publishing content around a small number of strategic themes. By focusing on core topics such as product expertise, industry trends, and customer challenges, SaaS marketers can strengthen their authority and improve the likelihood that their content reaches relevant decision-makers.
