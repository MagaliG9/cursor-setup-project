Pierre Herubel is a B2B content strategist and co-founder of Content Path. He specializes in helping B2B companies build authority through founder-led and team-led content programs. His work focuses on creating scalable content systems, leveraging visual content, and developing strategic frameworks that turn expertise into audience growth and pipeline generation. He is particularly known for his views on authority-first marketing and the evolution of LinkedIn as a primary channel for B2B growth.

## Post 1

**Title:** A Lot of Founders Are Starting Content Creation Wrong

### Why It Matters for B2B SaaS

Many SaaS founders struggle to differentiate themselves in crowded markets. Sharing firsthand experiences, lessons learned, and unique perspectives can build credibility and establish authority more effectively than generic marketing content. Similar themes appear in Pierre's guidance that experience-based insights are what help creators stand out in the AI era.

## Post 2

**Title:** In 2026 Social Content Becomes an SEO Asset

**Content Type:** LinkedIn Post

**Date:** March 2026

**Source:** [LinkedIn Profile](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/pierre-herubel-540b3949_in-2026-social-content-becomes-an-seo-asset-share-7427258604241567744-S4H5/)

### Summary

Pierre discusses how social media content is evolving beyond engagement and becoming a searchable knowledge asset. He argues that well-structured content can continue generating value long after publication through search visibility, content repurposing, and audience discovery.

### Main Marketing Lesson

Content should be created as a long-term business asset rather than a short-lived social media update.

### Why It Matters for B2B SaaS

B2B SaaS companies often invest heavily in content but focus only on immediate engagement metrics. Treating social content as a searchable asset can increase long-term visibility, authority, and lead generation opportunities.

## Post 3

**Title:** Choose Your Content Motion for 2026

**Content Type:** LinkedIn Post

**Date:** May 2026

**Source:** [LinkedIn Profile](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/pierre-herubel-540b3949_this-question-will-shape-your-content-strategy-share-7455873872165527552-kktu/)

### Summary

Pierre introduces four content motions that organizations can adopt: founder-led, team-led, brand-led, and community-led. He explains that each approach requires different workflows, resources, and strategic priorities. He also suggests that many early-stage B2B companies naturally progress from founder-led content toward brand and team-led approaches as they grow.

### Main Marketing Lesson

The most effective content strategy depends on organizational structure, resources, and business maturity

### Why It Matters for B2B SaaS

Many SaaS companies attempt to scale content without defining who should be creating it. Pierre's framework helps companies select the right content operating model and understand the bottlenecks associated with each approach.
