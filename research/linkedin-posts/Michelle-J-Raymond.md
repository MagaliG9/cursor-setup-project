## Post 3

**Title:** Content Formats and the Algorithm: The "No Think" Playbook

**Content Type:** Conference Insights / LinkedIn Post

**Date:** 2025

**Source:** [Business Gold Podcast](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/michellejraymond_b2bmarketing-linkedin-b2bgrowthnohacks-activity-7338797585077346304-IO4F)

### Summary

In her "No Think Content for LinkedIn" framework, Michelle explored how content format influences performance on the platform. She shared recommendations on ideal post length, explaining that thought-leadership posts perform best between 300 and 400 words, while very short posts often receive significantly lower distribution. She also challenged common practices such as placing links in comments, arguing that relevant native links can perform effectively when used strategically. Finally, she emphasized the importance of presenting information in highly digestible formats such as listicles, infographics, and actionable playbooks.

### Main Marketing Lesson

Content should be designed for clarity and ease of consumption. Marketers who package information into practical, easy-to-understand formats are more likely to earn engagement and build authority.

### Why It Matters for B2B SaaS

B2B SaaS companies often communicate complex products and technical concepts. Michelle's recommendations show how thoughtful formatting and content structure can improve engagement, making it easier for potential buyers to understand value propositions and take action.
