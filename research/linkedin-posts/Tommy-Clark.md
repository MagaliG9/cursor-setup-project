**Source:** [LinkedIn Profile](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tclarkmedia_ive-been-posting-on-linkedin-for-7-years-activity-7342551185926627328-VVRa)

### Summary

Tommy reflects on seven years of publishing content on LinkedIn and shares lessons learned from using the platform to grow his business. He emphasizes that audience building is a long-term process that requires consistency, patience, and a focus on attracting the right people rather than pursuing vanity metrics. He also highlights the importance of strong hooks, authentic content, and giving readers a clear next step after consuming content.

### Main Marketing Lesson

Building an audience on LinkedIn is a long-term investment. Sustainable growth comes from consistency, relevance, and focusing on high-quality relationships rather than maximizing follower counts.

### Why It Matters for B2B SaaS

Many B2B SaaS founders expect immediate results from content marketing and abandon their efforts too early. Tommy's experience demonstrates that consistent publishing can create trust and brand recognition over time, ultimately leading to stronger demand generation and business growth.
